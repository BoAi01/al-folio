Bo Ai

I am currently a research engineer at the Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore, working with Cheston Tan. I have also been collaborating with Jiajun Wu and Yunzhu Li. I am interested in learning dynamics models, sim-to-real transfer, and integrating learning and planning.

Earlier, I was a visiting researcher intern at the Stanford Vision and Learning Lab. I received my Bachelor’s in CS from the National University of Singapore, where I worked with David Hsu on long-horizon visual navigation with a Spot robot. I am joining UC San Diego as a PhD student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department in 2024 fall, working with Hao Su and Henrik I. Christensen.
